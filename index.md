## About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at the University at Buffalo (SUNY). My advisor is <a href="https://zzm7000.github.io/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Prof. Ziming Zhao</a>. My background is mainly in embedded firmware development and system security, especially low-level C programming, debugging, and performance analysis on microcontrollers.

## Skills

- **Embedded Systems:** Microcontroller firmware development, ARM Cortex-M architecture, real-time operating systems (RTOS), low-level C programming
- **Bus Protocols:** I2C, UART, SPI, CAN
- **Low-level Debugging:** GDB, J-Link (JTAG/SWD), J-Trace (ETM tracing), oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, ChipWhisperer
- **Edge AI:** PyTorch, model optimization for microcontrollers, on-device inference
